In five years I want to be measuring the right things. Right now I measure
shipped subsystems and weeks-of-runway, which are necessary but myopic.

Five years out, the metric I care about is the number of households whose
weekly time use shifted because of an artifact I helped build — not as a
proxy for reach, but as a proxy for *whether the thing is doing what it
was supposed to do.*

To get there I need:

- Prototype 3 in shippable form, manufacturable in batches of 100.
- A small permanent team — three, maybe five.
- A second-mover that I helped raise, working on the orthogonal axis.

I revise this annually; this is the 2025 cut.
