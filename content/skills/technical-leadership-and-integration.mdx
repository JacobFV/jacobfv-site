The integration layer is where product intent, engineering reality, and team
communication meet. I am comfortable owning that layer: contracts, schemas,
runtime boundaries, client expectations, and the documentation needed to keep
the system legible.

This is especially valuable when the product is still changing and the team
needs an architecture that can survive the next turn.
